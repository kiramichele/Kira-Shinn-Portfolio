01 — Overview

Cyberpunk Noir
Meets Tactical
Open World

"A disgraced detective navigates a rain-soaked megacity where every ally has a price and every choice rewrites the city's fate."
NEONRIFT is a 30-40 hour open-world action RPG set in a dystopian megacity controlled by warring corporate factions. Players build a web of alliances, expose conspiracies, and reshape the city through gameplay — not just dialogue trees.

03 — Visual Direction

Neon-Drenched
Noir. No
Compromises.

NEONRIFT's visual language blends the rain-soaked grit of classic noir film with hyper-saturated neon signage and brutalist megastructure architecture. Think Blade Runner meets Akira — photographed at 3 AM in the rain.
🌆

Procedural Weather System

Dynamic rain, fog, and neon reflection rendering via UE5 Lumen. Weather shifts faction patrol patterns and player visibility.

🎭

MetaHuman Character Fidelity

All major characters built in Unreal MetaHuman Creator. Micro-expression system drives trust and suspicion animations in real-time.

🏗️

4 Distinct Districts

Neon Bazaar, The Underplex, Corporate Citadel, and the Shallows — each with unique visual grammar and faction control.

🎮

Minimalist, Diegetic UI

Heads-up information lives in the world — projected on walls, wrist implants, and AR contact lenses. Zero HUD clutter.
