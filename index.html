About

Where creativity
meets rigor.

I'm a STEM educator and curriculum designer with a background that doesn't fit neatly into one box — and I think that's the point. With dual degrees in Applied Mathematics and Education from Marshall University and five-plus years of fully virtual teaching experience, I bring analytical precision and creative vision into everything I build.

At Georgia Cyber Academy, I teach Game Design: Animation & Simulation and Introduction to Software Technology to 125+ students. I design every lesson, project, and assessment from scratch — and use data analysis of student performance to continuously improve the curriculum. My classroom is a place where students write real C# code, build Unity games, and discover that math is everywhere.

Outside the classroom, I build things: WooCommerce stores, e-commerce websites with Stripe and Firestore, interactive learning tools, Kanban apps, handmade ceramics — and the occasional Python data pipeline. I believe the best instructional designers are people who never stopped being learners themselves.
